THE LAST 6 FUNCTIONS ARE REQUIRED BC THEY FUFILL OUR COMPLEX AND MEDIUM FUNCTION COMPLEXITY !!!!!!!!!!!!

validate_isbn(isbn) – checks if ISBN is valid length & format.
format_book_title(title) – trims whitespace, capitalizes words.
normalize_author_name(name) – formats names like “Last, First”.
is_valid_email(email) – basic check for “@” and “.” in email.
calculate_late_fee(days_late, rate) – returns overdue fee.
is_available(book_id) – checks if book is currently borrowed.
get_due_date(checkout_date, loan_period) – adds days to date.
format_date(date_obj) – outputs dates in a standard format.
generate_user_id(name) – makes a simple ID (e.g., initials + random number).
count_books(catalog) – returns total number of books.
find_book_by_id(book_id, catalog) – returns book object if found.
print_book_info(book) – nicely displays book details.
check_password_strength(password) – checks length + characters.
clean_input(text) – trims spaces and removes extra characters.
add_new_book(book_data, catalog) – validates & inserts book.
checkout_book(user_id, book_id, catalog, users) – updates user record & catalog status.
return_book(user_id, book_id, catalog, users) – handles book return and calculates late fees.
search_books(query, books_list) – supports searching by title, author, or ISBN.
generate_monthly_report(catalog, users) – shows stats (e.g., most borrowed, overdue counts).
import_books_from_csv(filename, catalog) – reads CSV, validates rows, and adds to catalog.
